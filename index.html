Services & Rates

Having a hard time keeping up with your pet's daily dog walk routine? Let us walk the walk for you! Our professional, loving dog walkers will come to your home, leash up your furry friend and take them on a walk with our packs.

Walking

Dogs need exercise and instinctively love to socialize with each other. Our fun-filled, closely supervised, group walks combine lots of exercise with a terrific opportunity to socialize. Your pup is walked with up to seven other companions and often your dog will be walked with only three or four other dogs. Your pup is never rushed and with trained eyes, we always know what he or she is sniffing and up too. Of course we never leave our dogs unattended for any reason.

Our Owner

Sabrina Hall

Sabrina was raised in San Jose and moved to San Francisco several years ago for school. After working in the environmental field, she decided it was time for a change in late 2014. She had always loved dogs and thought she could offer a superior dog-walking and dog-sitting service in San Francisco. The focus was to be on quality of care, not the quantity of the dogs. Bark Brigade not only stresses exercise and training but also ensures your dog has a great time!
